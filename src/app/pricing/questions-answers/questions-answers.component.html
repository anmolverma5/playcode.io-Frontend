Write us to online chat, we will send you an invoice or a payment request.
How can we pay, and how are we billed?
Billing happens on the date of the subscription – each month or each year – depending on whether you choose a monthly or annual subscription. You will get a receipt on your email.
Is our data secure?
We understand the sensitive and proprietary nature of the projects that our customers create. As a result, we encrypt all our communication through SSL. All data is stored securely, and backed up regularly in multiple geographic locations.
What happens if we want to change plans?
You can downgrade at any time, and there are no hidden fees. If you downgrade, your subscription remains valid for the period you’ve already paid for. And, you can upgrade when you need to – and regain access to our Premium features.
